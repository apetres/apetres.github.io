About us


The "Csorgó-patak Association" was found in 2008 at Csíkmadaras, Harghita county, Romania. The main objectives of the association are rural development and the protection of the unique local enviroment. Also we are focused especially on the young generation. We involve them into our projects so they can meet the huge natural values of their homeland.
